Finance Project

  • This webpage will allow you to simulate a trading strategy where you buy a stock of the ticker symbol you submit whenever the the 10-day Moving Average (MA-10) is higher than 50-day Moving Average (MA-50). In technical analysis, it is believed by some traders that if MA-10 is greater than MA-50, the stock has the potential to go up in the next several days.

  • When you submit the ticker symbol, the backend code will calculate the MA-10 and MA-50 of the stock price for the past 6 months and emulate buying and holding a stock whenever MA-10 is greater than MA-50.
